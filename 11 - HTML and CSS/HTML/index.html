US Company Net Profit Margin (NPM) Analysis Based on NPM %

This project’s purpose was to analyze the most and least profitable US publicy traded companies by Net Profit Margin (NPM) percentage from 2015 – 2017 based on the following criteria

Total Revenue greater than $6.5B / year

493 companies fit the criteria outlined above. This site provides source data and several visualizations developed as part of the analysis.
